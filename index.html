About me

Designing with purpose,
building with passion.

I'm Hiten Rajguru — a multidisciplinary designer who lives at the intersection of aesthetics and usability. Whether it's a full website, a mobile app flow, or a brand identity, my goal is the same: design that works as beautifully as it looks.

I believe great design is invisible — it guides, delights and never gets in the way. Every project starts with understanding people, then shaping ideas into clean, functional and memorable experiences.
